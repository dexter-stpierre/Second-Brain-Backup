- A weekly review isn’t only a retrospective, but a prospective too. It lets you run through the upcoming Monday to Friday and get prepared in advance. You’ll start each day armed with the game plan that you created during your weekly review.
  Proactively planning for the week ahead doesn’t mean scheduling every single thing you’ll need to do. Instead, it means laying out your top priorities so they have visibility in your calendar or task list. This approach helps you get out of reactive mode and get ahead of your to-dos in a way that aligns with your long-term goals.
